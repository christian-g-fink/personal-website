I have enjoyed running competitively since I was ten years old. I love the simplicity and the quantifiability of running: the goal is to finish in less time than you ever have before, and the stopwatch doesn't lie. Below is a list of my personal records in several distances. The picture to the right was taken after I broke my PR in the mile, a few months before turning 30 (the age at which I was warned that "the warranty runs out").
