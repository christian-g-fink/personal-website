My wife, Chrissie, and I live in Spokane, WA, where we enjoy so many wonderful parks, mountains, and lakes. In my spare time I enjoy running, hiking, skiing, traveling, reading, and learning Chinese (I took a gap year after college to teach in China). I also enjoy cheering for the Michigan football team. Some of my favorite books include The Brothers Karamazov, Love in the Time of Cholera, The Varieties of Religious Experience, The Making of the Atomic Bomb, and Consciousness: Confessions of a Romantic Reductionist. Check out my Goodreads profile for my reviews of these and other books.
