I am a professor of physics at Gonzaga University, where I teach a variety of courses across the physics curriculum. My interests are quite interdisciplinary, as I have previously developed courses in Introductory Physics for the Life Sciences, Computational Neuroscience, and Data Science. In my research I apply tools and ways of thinking from physics to better understand the brain. I am especially interested in better understanding how epileptic seizures are generated and how they may be controlled.
